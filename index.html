Our Products

Pusa (D.B) Basmati Rice

Experience the finest quality Basmati rice, known for its exquisite aroma and long, fluffy grains. Our premium Basmati is sourced from the best paddy fields, ensuring perfect texture and taste for your gourmet dishes.

Origin: Developed by the Indian Agricultural Research Institute in New Delhi, India.

First Cultivated: Introduced in the early 1990s as an improved Basmati variety.

Characteristics: Extra-long grains, distinctive aroma, and excellent cooking quality.

PR 11 White Sella Rice

Discover our premium PR 11 White Sella rice, a popular variety known for its excellent quality and versatility. Perfect for everyday meals, this rice offers a delightful texture and taste that complements a wide range of dishes.

Origin: Developed in Punjab, India, by the Punjab Agricultural University.

First Cultivated: Introduced in the mid-1980s as a high-yielding variety.

Characteristics: Medium-long grains, slightly aromatic, and ideal for both everyday and special occasion cooking.

Parboiled Broken Rice

Our parboiled broken rice is a nutritious and economical option. With its unique processing method, it retains more nutrients than regular white rice while offering a satisfying texture, making it ideal for various culinary applications.

Origin: Parboiling is an ancient process originating in India over 2000 years ago.

Process: Rice is partially boiled in the husk, changing the starch structure and increasing nutrient retention.

Benefits: Higher in nutrients, especially B vitamins, compared to regular milled rice. Excellent for making rice flour and animal feed.

PR 106 Parboiled Rice

Our PR 106 Parboiled Rice offers the perfect balance of nutrition and taste. This variety is known for its ability to remain separate after cooking, making it an excellent choice for a wide range of dishes, from biryanis to everyday meals.

Origin: Developed by Punjab Agricultural University, Ludhiana, India.

First Cultivated: Released in 1976 as a high-yielding variety.

Characteristics: Medium-long grains, slightly sticky when cooked, and rich in nutrients due to the parboiling process.

IR 64 Parboiled Rice with 5% Broken

Our IR 64 Parboiled Rice with 5% Broken offers great value without compromising on quality. This variety is perfect for those seeking a nutritious and cost-effective rice option that performs well in various cooking methods.

Origin: Developed by the International Rice Research Institute (IRRI) in the Philippines.

First Cultivated: Released in 1985 and quickly became popular across Asia.

Characteristics: Medium-long grains, good cooking quality, and disease resistance. The 5% broken content adds value while maintaining overall quality.

1121 Stream Basmati Rice

Indulge in the premium quality of our 1121 Stream Basmati Rice. Known for its extra-long grains and aromatic flavor, this rice variety is perfect for special occasions and gourmet dishes, elevating your culinary creations to new heights.

Origin: Developed by Indian Agricultural Research Institute (IARI), New Delhi, India.

First Cultivated: Introduced in 2003 as a high-yielding, extra-long grain Basmati variety.

Characteristics: Extra-long grains (up to 8.4mm after cooking), distinctive aroma, and excellent elongation upon cooking. Known for its soft texture and delicate flavor.
